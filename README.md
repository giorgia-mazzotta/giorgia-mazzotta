### Space Mission and Systems (Courseworks)
* **Orbit Determination via Weighted Least-Squares Filter:** Implementation of an orbit determination process using a weighted least-squares algorithm with *a priori* information to estimate a spacecraft's state vector and uncertainties around Mars. The analysis includes covariance matrix propagation and the evaluation of atmospheric drag perturbations.
* **Attitude Control System Design for Lunar Gravity Assist:** Design of a time-variable PD attitude control system utilizing reaction wheels to maintain nadir pointing during a Lunar flyby. The project includes gravity gradient torque evaluation and the design of a thruster-based wheel desaturation maneuver.

### Space Structures (Courseworks)
* **Ariane 5 Solid Rocket Booster Sizing:** Conducted a preliminary structural sizing and material trade-off analysis for the Ariane 5 boosters. Evaluated the Margin of Safety (MOS) and structural mass under gravitational, inertial, and pressurization loads, comparing various high-strength steel and titanium alloys.
* **Spacecraft Reticular Structure Optimization:** Analyzed and sized a truss structure connecting propellant tanks to a spacecraft's central body. Performed static equilibrium analysis and iterative optimization of cross-sectional geometries to minimize structural mass while ensuring compliance with tensile yielding and compressive buckling constraints.
* **1D Finite Element Method (FEM) Implementation:** Developed a 1D FEM framework to solve the displacement and strain fields of a fixed beam under sinusoidal loading. Analyzed the numerical convergence rate by comparing multiple linear and quadratic element discretizations against the exact analytical solution.
* **Simply Supported Plate Theory & Buckling:** Solved the governing differential equations for a simply supported aluminum plate using Navier's double Fourier series expansion. Evaluated the transverse displacement field under decoupled and coupled membrane-bending loads, and analytically determined the critical compressive load to prevent buckling instability.
